Introducing Sukkanya Ramgopal

The first woman ghatam artist of India and is one of the frontline musicians of the country today.

Profile

Born in Mayiladuthurai in Tamilnadu, Sukkanya hails from a family of musicians and Tamil scholars.

Gurus

Sukkanya is a disciple of ghatam maestro Padmabhushan Sri Vikku Vinayakram.

About Ghatam

The Ghatam is an ancient percussion instrument widely used in South Indian music. It is a clay pot with a narrow mouth.
