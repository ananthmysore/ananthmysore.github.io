Product Description: Shine, bounce and keep touching those locks after a intense moisturizing treatment. Lock the moisture in and nourish from the roots with our hair mask. All the goodness of gooseberry, curry leaves, fenugreek, hibiscus extracts, and orange to reinvigorate your hair the natural way. All natural aloe gel-promotes shine and moisture in hair, preventing hair loss and dandruff. Inspired by the luscious thick locks of those women with oh-so-long and beautiful hair in Raja Ravi Verma paintings.

Q Factor: All natural aloe gel-promotes shine and moisture in hair, and preventing hair loss and dandruff.

Material Used: Extracts of Fenugreek, Orange Peel, Hibiscus flower, Hibiscus leaf, Gooseberry, Curry leaves and Tulasi.

Benefits: Nourishing for Dry and damaged hair

Shelf Life: 12 months if not contaminated by water

Once Opened: Please refrigerate and mix together only when required.

How to Use: Mix a tablespoon of the hair mask powder with two tablespoons of the pure aloe gel. The thicker your hair the more you will need. If the aloe gel quantity is not enough please mix with curd. Apply onto hair and leave for at least 20 minutes before washing off.
